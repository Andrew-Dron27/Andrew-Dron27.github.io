About me

I am 25 years old and graduated from the University of Utah in 2020 with a bachelors in Computer Science. I have 4 years of professional work experience in the tech industry with 3 years as an intern working at Becton Dickinson, and 1 year as a software engineer at Sorenson Communications. My main experience is in developing user facing products that range from ultrasound needle tracking devices to web video services for sign language relay interpreters. I have most experience in C#, javascript and typescript, react, flutter, and C++ Currently in my free time I am studying the language Rust and its asynchronous libraries, so that I can use it for a restfull service that provides both perfromance and programatic saftey.

Outside of work I love cycling, rock climbing, snowboarding, and playing guitar. Last year I resigned from my full time position at Sorenson to pursue and adventurous opportunity to cycle across Norway with my cousin. We did about 3 weeks of bikepacking, and I spent the next 2 months backpacking through Europe. It was truly the adventure of a lifetime, and I wouldnt trade it for anything, but it explains my current situation of seeking employment.

I absolulty am confident in my abilites to perform as a software engineer in industry. I was consistently received grades at the top of my class and worked as an intern while going to school. However, my travels have left me a much more confident person better to not only deal with the technical side of software development, but also the people and communication side. I am more assured in my abilites to assist in the creation of complex programs and solving difficult problems than ever. I am just waiting for and opportunity to demonstrate that.
